### Now for some non-AI news!

Tesla's EV charging technology is on the fast track to become a US standard. Reuters [reports](https://www.reuters.com/business/autos-transportation/tesla-charging-technology-put-fast-track-become-us-industry-standard-2023-06-27/) that:

> SAE International aims to make an industry standard configuration of Tesla's charging connector in six months or less, an official at the standards organization said on Tuesday.

The Biden administration had previously tried to make the Combined Charging System (CCS) the standard, similar to the EU.

[Proton](https://proton.me) have released [Proton Pass](https://proton.me/pass), an open-source and encrypted password manager. It's nice to see them gradually building a suite of work tools as a privacy-first alternative to offerings by Big Tech. TechCrunch reviews it [here](https://techcrunch.com/2023/06/28/proton-launches-its-password-manager-proton-pass/).
