### OpenAI introduces GPT Store

    At OpenAI's first developer conference, Sam Altman [announced](https://openai.com/blog/introducing-gpts) a way to create custom AI bots and share them on the OpenAI platform.

  

![Image Credits: OpenAI GPT Store](https://techcrunch.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/11/GPT-Store.jpg?w=680)

They also announced a whole range of new features, and what better way to summarise it than by using their own tool? 😎

As you can see, ChatGPT no longer is limited to information from 2021 and the above UI told me how it browsed the web with Bing then read an article on [Decrypt](https://decrypt.co/204516/heres-everything-announced-at-openais-dev-day) in order to generate its response. This is no longer a separate feature like it was a few months ago—it's now integrated into the main product seamlessly.

You can compare what it generated with the coverage from [TechCrunch](https://techcrunch.com/2023/11/06/everything-announced-at-openais-first-developer-event/) and [The Verge](https://www.theverge.com/2023/11/6/23948619/openai-chatgpt-devday-developer-conference-news).
